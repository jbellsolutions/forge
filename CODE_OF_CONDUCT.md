# Code of Conduct

## TL;DR

Be the kind of collaborator you'd want on your team. We're a small project;
behave accordingly.

## Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a healthy environment:

- Showing empathy and kindness toward other contributors.
- Critiquing the code, not the person.
- Accepting constructive feedback gracefully.
- Focusing on what's best for the project and its users.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Personal attacks, insults, or derogatory comments.
- Trolling, harassment (public or private), or sustained disruption.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.
- Sexualized language, imagery, or unwelcome advances.
- Conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a
  professional setting.

## Scope

This Code applies in all project spaces (issues, PRs, discussions, related
chat) and when an individual is representing the project in public spaces.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior to **conduct@usingaitoscale.com**. All reports
are reviewed promptly and confidentially. Maintainers may warn, temporarily
ban, or permanently ban any contributor whose behavior violates this Code.
